If you were to meantion cryptocurrencies the first word that would come to mind is "bitcoin". It is true that bitcoin is the most well known cryptoocurrency, but when it comes to mining it may not be the most lucrative. For those are early adopters, bitcoin may have been an optimal choice, as mining in general benefits the early adopter. In todays market, however ROI on mining bitcion is most often negative. Alt-coins such as Litecoin which are newer to the marketplace, are easier to mine and require less intensive computing. On your journey 'mining' through this informative site, I hope you will leave with a richer understand of the industry as a whole ;) .
